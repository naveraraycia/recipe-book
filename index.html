Recipe List

  • Filipino Pancit Canton

    Ingredients

    Quantity

    Chicken Breast Fillet
    2 pcs
    Egg Noodles 400g
    1 pack
    Carrots
    2 pcs
    Onions
    2 pcs
    Garlic
    7 cloves
    Celery
    2 pcs
    Green Beans
    15 pcs
    Water
    4 cups
    Chicken Stock Cubes
    3 pcs
    Soy Sauce
    2 tbsp
    Black Ground Pepper
    2 tsp

    Directions

    1. Start with preparing the ingredients. Mince onion and garlic. Cut the carrots into thin slices, the celery and spring onions into small pieces, and the green beans into slanted cuts. For the chicken breast fillet, shred it into small pieces.

    2. Put 1/2 Cup of oil into wok and wait for it to reach the ideal temperature for sauteing. Saute onion and garlic then add carrots. Keep on mixing until the carrots become tender then add the green beans, celery, and shredded chicken.

    3. Set aside everything once fully cooked since you will use the same wok for cooking the noodles. After setting aside the vegetables with the chicken shreads, add 4 cups of water into wok and then the chicken stock cubes. Stir until the cubes are completely dissolved.

    4. Add the egg noodles and stir until you reach the ideal pasta texture based on your liking. The noodles usually gets cooked in 5 mins of stirring. Then add 4 tbsp of soy sauce onto cooked noodles and mix.

    5. Once everything is mixed, add the vegetables you set aside earlier and mix again. Then add the black ground pepper to taste as well as your spring onions. Stir until satisfied. Enjoy!

    Number of Servings: 2

    Cooking Time: 1hr
